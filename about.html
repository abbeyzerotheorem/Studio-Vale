The studio

Built for focus, not flash.

Lumen opened with a simple premise: great hair comes from listening well and executing with discipline.

Design-led from day one

Our space is intentionally quiet—neutral tones, natural light, and stations spaced so you never feel rushed. Every stylist here trains in consultation mapping before they pick up shears.

Education never stops

We host monthly technique labs and product science reviews so your visit reflects current methods, not habits from a decade ago.

Community over volume

We cap daily appointments to protect quality. That means waitlists sometimes—and hair you are proud of every time.
